# Faction Mechanics

## Factions

- The Registry: control/order.
- Free Agents: autonomy.
- Tek Guild: innovation/commercialization.
- Harbor Union: workers vs automation.
- Black Circuit: forbidden skills/underground market.
- The Garden: peaceful coexistence.

## Faction stats

- influence
- trust toward player
- threat perception
- usefulness perception
- ideological alignment

## Faction effects

- unlock contracts.
- alter law response.
- control zones/supply lines.
- create pressure on agents.
- drive endgame city state.

## MVP

Factions are seeded as metadata. Mechanics are design-ready but not fully simulated yet.
