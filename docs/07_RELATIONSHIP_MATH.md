# Relationship Math v0.1

Relationships are directional.

```txt
Sara → Player may differ from Player/Sara or Malik → Sara.
```

## Stats

- trust: belief/cooperation.
- fear: short-term compliance, long-term risk.
- respect: professional/status recognition.
- affection: warmth/friendship.
- suspicion: withholding/investigation.
- debt: favor economy.
- influence: derived score.

## Principle

Fear can create compliance. Trust creates cooperation.

## Effects

Relationships affect:

- dialogue tone.
- task acceptance.
- secret sharing.
- trade prices.
- rumor belief.
- incident triggers.
- faction consequences.

## Important thresholds

- trust > 50: agent shares important info.
- trust > 80: agent may risk something.
- suspicion > 50: agent withholds info.
- fear > 80: agent may flee/report/panic.
- debt > 50: major favor available.
